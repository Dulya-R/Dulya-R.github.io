Hotels in Sri Lanka

Scattered throughout the expanse of our tropical island, our Sri Lankan homes beckon you to savor our unique brand of luxury, fueled by the enduring strength of our cherished family values. Regardless of your location within Sri Lanka, rest assured that our doors stand wide open to welcome you.
As you embark on your journey to explore the marvels of our captivating island, you will come to realize that our unwavering commitment to gracious hospitality and time-honored traditions knows no limits. Whether you seek serenity on pristine beaches, embark on adventures in verdant jungles, or immerse yourself in our rich culture and cuisine, our residences will provide you with a sanctuary. Welcome to Sri Lanka, where our hospitality transforms your visit into an unforgettable experience.

Lavender Garden Galle

The Lavender Garden Hotel is a tranquil retreat in the heart of Galle, a picturesque city where serenity and traditional elegance meet. Our boutique hotel, nestled amidst scented lavender gardens, provides peaceful accommodations with tastefully appointed rooms and attentive staff.

Whether you prefer lounging by our inviting pool or strolling the cobblestone streets of Galle Fort, our hotel is the perfect base to discover the coastal charm of this beautiful city.

Lavender Garden Yala

The Lavender Garden Hotel in Yala, Sri Lanka, is a peaceful hideaway near Yala National Park, known for abundant wildlife like leopards and elephants.

Amidst lush gardens, this charming hotel offers cozy rooms, delightful dining, and a welcoming staff. Whether you seek tranquility or wildlife adventures, it's the perfect start for exploring Yala's natural beauty and making lasting memories.

Lavender Garden Kandy

Nestled in the scenic highlands of Kandy, Sri Lanka, the peaceful Lavender Garden Hotel offers stunning views of lush greenery.

Just a short drive from cultural gems like the Temple of the Tooth Relic, our charming guesthouse provides comfortable lodgings and delectable cuisine amidst a tranquil ambiance. It's the ideal base for exploring Kandy's rich heritage and natural wonders.
