An Exquisite Dining Experience

We have a strong passion for the culinary arts at Lavender Garden Hotels and Resorts, and we are committed to creating exceptional dishes with fresh, locally produced, and healthful ingredients. Because we use locally sourced products, whether you're dining in the chilly highlands or by the sea, the ingredients in your meal are as fresh as they can be. Our components are sourced from local farmers who cultivate their crops without the use of preservatives, as well as fisherman who provide a fresh catch every morning. When you dine at one of our restaurants, you embark on a culinary trip that narrates our dedication to authentic and evocative cuisine.

The Cannonball Restaurant

The Cannonball Restaurant, situated by the serene seaside, specializes in authentic Sri Lankan cuisine and the freshest seafood. With its picturesque oceanfront setting, it offers a delightful dining experience featuring aromatic curries, flavorful rice dishes, and a delectable array of locally sourced seafood.

The Velvet Lounge

The Velvet Lounge is a haven for drink enthusiasts, offering a wide selection of premium libations. From rare whiskies and crafted cocktails to vintage wines and artisanal spirits, our luxury bar caters to connoisseurs of all tastes. Experience liquid luxury in an elegant setting.

Jade Garden

Jade Garden is an East Asian culinary paradise, offering a diverse menu that celebrates the flavors of China, Thailand, and Japan. Experience an authentic dining adventure with vibrant decor and a wide range of dishes from sushi to stir-fries, all under one roof.

Coffee Break
