Lavender Garden

Hotels & Resorts

Welcome to Lavender Garden Hotels & Resorts, a collection of opulent hotels nestled in breathtaking natural locations with a fascinating cultural background. Our renowned hospitality is something we are quite proud of and will always be connected with our brand, wherever you may run into us on your far-flung travels. The greatest aspects of Sri Lankan hospitality and the surrounding natural beauty have been elegantly interwoven into the construction of our venues, creating unequaled levels of luxury. With rooms that pull you into a world of your own and signature dining experiences that transport you, your stay with us will be one to remember.

Hotels in Sri Lanka

Our homes of Sri Lankan hospitality are scattered around our tropical island and invite you to experience our unique brand of luxury, which is made possible by the tenacity of our deeply embedded family values.

Our hotels in Sri Lanka were inspired by a variety of magnificent locales that exude an innate feeling of warmth and caring, that has been nurtured through decades.

Weddings and Events

Lavender Garden Hotels & Resorts offers a range of unique event spaces in each of our distinctive properties.

Whether it's a beachside conference at Lavender Garden Galle, a peaceful getaway at Lavender Garden Yala, or a dream wedding in the highlands at Lavender Garden Kandy, our venues provide the perfect backdrop for your event.

Delectable Cuisine

At Lavender Garden Hotels & Resorts, we prioritize culinary excellence, utilizing locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients for our delectable dishes.

We meticulously plan to source ingredients locally for our guests' meals, whether in the hills or by the seaside Our restaurant experience tells a captivating story,starting with dedicated farmers and fishermen who provide fresh, preservative-free ingredients.
